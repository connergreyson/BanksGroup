Meet The Banks Group

Native Austinites with a deep passion for family, faith, and helping people find their place to call home.

Marty Banks and his wife, Tracy, grew up in Austin and have witnessed the city's incredible growth and transformation firsthand. Their love for this vibrant community runs deep, and they take pride in being a part of its evolution. As real estate professionals, they strive to contribute to the fabric of the city by assisting individuals and families in finding their dream homes or selling their properties for new beginnings.

For Marty and Tracy, real estate is far more than a transaction—it's a calling rooted in their love for people. They genuinely care about the well-being and happiness of their clients and are committed to providing exceptional service and guidance throughout the entire process. Their faith plays a central role in their lives, and they serve their community with compassion, integrity, and a genuine desire to make a positive impact.

As a family-oriented couple, they understand the importance of finding a place to call home where lasting memories can be made. When you choose The Banks Group as your real estate partners, you can expect a personalized and attentive approach. Marty and Tracy take the time to listen to your needs, understand your unique circumstances, and tailor their services to exceed expectations. From finding the ideal neighborhood to negotiating the best deal, they will be by your side every step of the way.
